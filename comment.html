How should I train for distance running?
Training for distance running typically involves a mix of long runs, speed work, tempo runs, and recovery runs. Long runs build endurance, speed work improves pace, and tempo runs help increase lactate threshold. A well-rounded training plan also includes rest days and cross training to prevent injury.
